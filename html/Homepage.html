Hello, world!

This blog is about beauty, fashion, lifestyle and more. We strongly believes that fashion is an art and is inspired by the myriad of colours.To be a fashion blogger it is important to have great content writing, aesthetic, creative, visualisation, marketing, communication, time-management, multi-tasking, critical, social networking skills. fashion bloggers and fashion stylists are very different. Fashion bloggers use social media to channelise their love for fashion. They recommend, write and analyse the fashion trends to the readers. A fashion stylist, on the other hand, works for individuals, fashion houses and clothing brands and earns by providing them fashion consultancy as per the individual style of the client.
